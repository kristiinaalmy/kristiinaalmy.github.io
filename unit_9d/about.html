About Kristiina

Hello! I'm an experienced designer and artist, based in greater Boston, Massachusetts, specializing in graphic design and oil painting. I have had the great pleasure of working with all types of clients, from start-up businesses to global brands as well as artists and non-profits. When I'm not working on exciting new design projects, I like to spend my time painting landscapes or still lives on canvas and designing images for retail projects. I am also a 13 year supporter of the Dana Farber Cancer Institute, by fundraising for and riding in the PMC bike-a-thon. I enjoy spending my down time traveling and exploring, locally and afar.
